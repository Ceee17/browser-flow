## Architecture Decisions

- Selector engine separates resolution from scoring. Resolution is synchronous against a Playwright-style locator interface.
- Browser engine exposes an operation-per-method interface rather than a single execute method. This keeps testing trivial.
- Scheduler defines trigger types, not execution logic. The actual cron queue integration (BullMQ) will live in `apps/api` or a future `packages/scheduler-queue`.
- Control flow steps (condition/loop/error) are intentionally excluded from browser-engine supported actions; they are handled by the workflow engine.
